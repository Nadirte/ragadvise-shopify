# App Store listing copy

## App name  (≤30 chars)

RagAdvise Site Assistant

## App introduction  (≤100 chars — the one-line summary on the app card)

Add an AI assistant to your storefront that answers shopper questions 24/7 — no theme code.

## App details  (≤500 chars — the main description)

RagAdvise Site Assistant adds an AI chat assistant to your store that answers product and order questions, helps shoppers find what they need, and can book appointments — around the clock. Install in one click: enable the app embed in your theme editor and paste your RagAdvise workspace ID. It loads on every page with a lightweight script that won't slow your store. The assistant is trained on your own knowledge base and fully configured from your RagAdvise dashboard.

## Key benefits  (3 × title ≤40 chars + description ≤100 chars)

1. **One-click install**
   Enable it in your theme editor — no Liquid or code. Live on every page instantly.

2. **Answers in your brand voice**
   Trained on your RagAdvise knowledge base and configured from your dashboard.

3. **Fast and free**
   A lightweight loader script that won't slow your storefront down.
